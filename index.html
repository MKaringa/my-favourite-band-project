This is my band, Morgan Heritage.

Let us get to know them.

Morgan Heritage, formed in 1994 by five children of reggae artist Denroy Morgan, namely:

  • Nakhamyah "Lukes" Morgan
  • Memmalatel "Mr. Mojo" Morgan
  • Roy "Gramps" Morgan
  • Peter "Peetah" Morgan
  • Una Morgan

2 of these members Una Morgan and Lukes left the band after relocating to Denroy's homeland in Jamaica. It was during this period that they launched their first album Miracle
